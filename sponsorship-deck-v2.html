The Event

A Global Chess Championship Comes to Hong Kong — For the First Time

June 16–22, 2026  |  Queen Elizabeth Stadium

This June, Hong Kong will host the FIDE World Team Rapid & Blitz Chess Championships — the first major global chess championship ever held in Hong Kong. The event brings together teams from around the world to compete for a €500,000 prize fund at Queen Elizabeth Stadium.

Hong Kong joins Düsseldorf, Astana, and London as host cities of this prestigious championship — putting our city on the global chess map.
