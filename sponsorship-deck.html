The Event

A Global Chess Championship Comes to Hong Kong — For the First Time

This June, Hong Kong will host the FIDE World Team Rapid & Blitz Chess Championships — the first major global chess championship ever held in Hong Kong. Teams from around the world will compete for a €500,000 prize fund at Queen Elizabeth Stadium.

The world's top grandmasters are expected to compete, including Magnus Carlsen (Norway) — the highest-rated player in history — and Ding Liren (China) — the first-ever Chinese World Chess Champion.
