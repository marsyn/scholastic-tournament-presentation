The Event

Hong Kong's First Global Chess Championship

FIDE World Team Rapid & Blitz Championships 2026

This June, Hong Kong will host the FIDE World Team Rapid & Blitz Chess Championships — the first major global chess championship ever held in Hong Kong. The event brings together teams from around the world to compete for a €500,000 prize fund at Queen Elizabeth Stadium. Hong Kong joins Düsseldorf, Astana, and London as host cities of this prestigious championship — putting our city on the global chess map.
